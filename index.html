About our Club

The University of Manitoba Campus Conservatives gathers conservative-minded students at the University of Manitoba, promote the principles of fiscal responsibility, limited government and respect for tradition amongst the student body. We welcome all levels of participation, from casual supporters to die-hard partisans. Our events offer an opportunity for conservative-minded students to connect, whether over food and drink or with talks from elected officials and other influential figures within the movement.
